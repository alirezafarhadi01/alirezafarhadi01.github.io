Publications

Brains vs. Bytes: Evaluating LLM Proficiency in Olympiad Mathematics

accepted at Conference on Language Modeling (COLM 2025)
PDF

LLMs still struggle on Olympiad math. Human reviews and an automatic checker show shortcut answers, not real proofs. We need benchmarks that reward sound reasoning over final answers.

RefGrader: Automated Grading of Mathematical Competition Proofs using Agentic Workflows

accepted at NeurIPS 2025 Workshop MATH-AI
PDF

Top LLMs now solve most IMO 2025 problems. We test their proof grading: great at spotting errors, inconsistent on partial credit. Agentic workflows build rubrics, boosting fairness and human-level agreement.
